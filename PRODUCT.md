# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People who want to build and maintain daily habits — self-improvement oriented, motivated but not always consistent. They open the app at the start or end of their day to log progress, review streaks, and follow guided programs. Context: low ambient pressure, personal goal-setting, often mobile.

## Product Purpose

HabitFlow lets users track daily habits, follow structured multi-day programs, and visualize their consistency over time. Success looks like a user who checks in daily without friction and finishes a program they started.

## Brand Personality

Focused, precise, motivating. The app earns trust through calm clarity, not through gamification noise. It respects the user's time and doesn't shout for attention.

## Anti-references

- Generic health/wellness apps (MyFitnessPal-style: heavy greens, calorie counters, body metrics)
- Gamified productivity apps (Duolingo-style: streaks celebrated with confetti, mascots, excessive praise)
- Minimal note-taking tools (Notion-style: too editorial, no visual energy)
- SaaS cream/sand aesthetic: warm-neutral backgrounds are the default AI move and explicitly avoided here

## Design Principles

1. Calm over excitement — state changes are acknowledged, not celebrated. Motion serves feedback, not spectacle.
2. Consistency earns trust — the same visual vocabulary screen to screen. A button that looks different in two places is a bug.
3. Depth without decoration — glassmorphism and glow are used because the dark UI earns them, not as default style.
4. Responsive by structure — layout adapts at breakpoints; typography stays fixed; density is appropriate to context.
5. Accessibility is baseline — WCAG AA contrast, 44px touch targets, visible focus rings, reduced-motion respect.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Both dark and light themes must meet contrast requirements. Touch targets 44×44px minimum. Reduced motion preference respected via Framer Motion's MotionConfig. Focus rings always visible (never removed without replacement).
